Quick FAQs

How does consent work?

Teens enroll with a parent/guardian. Parents review/approve involvement and can opt to be present at jobs.

What about Virginia work permits?

Under 16? Virginia requires a Youth Employment Certificate before paid work. We’ll guide you through the steps.

What if a task seems unsafe?

Your teen or you can decline any task. We encourage open communication and will address concerns promptly.
