About UpNext Works

UpNext Works connects families with trusted teen taskers for neighborhood jobs — from yardwork and snow shoveling to babysitting, pet care, and tutoring. We’re helping young adults gain hands-on experience, build responsibility, and earn safely within their community.

Our Mission

We believe in empowering the next generation of workers while providing families with dependable local help. By creating a structured, parent-supported system, we’re bridging the gap between youth readiness and community needs.

Safety & Oversight

Every teen profile is verified and linked to a parent or guardian account. Tasks are matched according to age-appropriate guidelines and safety standards. Our in-app features include:

  • ✅ Verified identities and parental oversight
  • ✅ Secure in-app payments once work is completed
  • ✅ Safety checklists and built-in task approvals
  • ✅ Automated labor law compliance screening by age and jurisdiction

Why It Matters

Today’s teens often lack structured opportunities to work locally and safely. UpNext Works fills that gap — providing real-world experience under family guidance and community support. Customers get reliable help; teens gain skills, income, and purpose.
