Why UpNext Works

We’re building a safe, local way for families to get help — and for teens to gain real-world experience — with clear guardrails and oversight baked in.

The need we’re filling

  • ✓ Families need trustworthy, short-duration help for everyday home tasks.
  • ✓ Teens need safe, guided, flexible ways to earn and learn without risky gig platforms.
  • ✓ UpNext is the first to integrate safety, compliance, and parental consent end-to-end.

Safety and compliance, by design

  • ✓ Verified teen and parent/guardian accounts.
  • ✓ Age-appropriate task matching and hour limits built into the app.
  • ✓ Secure communication, safety checklists, and task verification.
  • ✓ Automatic adherence to youth-labor laws — no guessing required.
