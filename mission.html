Why Workish?

Teens get real-world experience and income. Parents get oversight. Neighbors get the simple help they need.

The gap we’re closing

  • Teen income & responsibility. Teens want to earn, but options are limited, irregular, or unsafe. Workish creates a path to earn locally with clear expectations and receipts for the real world.
  • Parent oversight that didn’t exist. Traditional “knock and ask” jobs leave parents in the dark. With Workish, parents approve tasks, see messages, set curfews/hour limits, and can require check-ins or presence.
  • A marketplace for everyday jobs. Homeowners often don’t need a full service company—just raking, walkway shoveling, pet care, or help carrying boxes. Workish makes those tasks easy to request and easy to say yes to.
  • Community-first. Neighbors hire neighbors. Earnings stay local. Teens learn professionalism and customer service in low-risk, age-appropriate work.

Who it’s for

Parents & guardians
  • Approve your teen’s profile, tasks, and schedule.
  • See every message; require on-site adult; set curfews.
  • Automatic receipts and activity history.
Teen taskers (14–17)
  • Start with safe, age-appropriate work that builds confidence.
  • Track earnings, goals, and streaks; unlock badges.
  • Practice real-world professionalism—show up, communicate, deliver.
Homeowners & neighbors
  • Hire for smaller jobs without the headache of full services.
  • Transparent rates, timing windows, and on-platform payment.
  • Feel good supporting local teens with parent oversight.

Safety & oversight—baked in

  • Linked teen–parent accounts with approvals, curfews, and hour caps.
  • Verified adults and clear homeowner standards (on-site adult, safe equipment, potable water, weather breaks).
  • Age-appropriate tasks only. Hazardous categories are blocked by design.
  • On-platform communication with exportable receipts; optional location check-ins.
  • Report & pause controls if anything feels off.
