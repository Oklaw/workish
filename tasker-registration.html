Parental Consent Notice
Participation in Kindra's pilot is limited to teens ages 14–17. A parent or legal guardian must complete this form to grant consent for their teen’s involvement. After approval, the teen will receive an individual registration link and Terms of Service.

Virginia Youth Work Permit Requirement: Teens under 16 years old are required by Virginia law to obtain a valid Employment Certificate (Work Permit) before beginning any paid work. Parents or guardians must approve the application, and the employer (Kindra) must also provide information as part of the form.
