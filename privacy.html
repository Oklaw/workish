Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Workish, Inc. (“Workish,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects personal information when you use our platform (the “Platform”).

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth.
  • Parental Information: Parent/guardian name, email, phone number, and consent verification details.
  • Profile Information: Skills, availability, service categories.
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through third-party payment providers (e.g., Stripe). Workish does not store full payment card numbers.
  • Location Information: ZIP code and, if permitted, browser-based geolocation data.
  • Usage Data: IP address, device information, browser type, pages visited.
  • Communications: Messages sent through the Platform.

2. Minimum Age

Workish is intended for users age 14 and older.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 14. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

3. Information About Minors (Ages 14–17)

For users under 18:

  • Parental consent is required before account activation.
  • We collect only information reasonably necessary to operate the Platform.
  • Parents may request review, correction, or deletion of their minor’s information.

Parents may contact us at the email listed below to exercise these rights.

4. How We Use Information

We use personal information to:

  • Provide and operate the Platform
  • Verify parental consent
  • Facilitate payments
  • Improve platform functionality
  • Communicate updates and safety notices
  • Detect fraud or misuse

5. Sharing of Information

We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors, hosting providers, analytics tools.
  • Other Users: Limited profile information necessary to arrange services.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect safety.

We do not sell personal information.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information.

However, no system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain information as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

8. Virginia Privacy Rights

If you are a Virginia resident, you may have rights under the Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act (VCDPA), including the right to:

  • Access personal data
  • Correct inaccuracies
  • Request deletion
  • Obtain a copy of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.

9. Cookies & Analytics

We may use cookies or similar technologies to improve user experience and analyze traffic.

You can adjust browser settings to decline cookies.

10. Third-Party Links

The Platform may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the Platform constitutes acceptance of updates.
